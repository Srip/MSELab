Cloud computing : 
	1.Allows you to store and access all your files and folders in the "cloud" area on the internet.
	2.Provides virtualization and the concept of wherever and whenever.
	3.A physical device with internet access is the requirement.
	4.The computing power is more than that of the physical device at hand as a result of virtualization.
	5.Cloud computing provides scope for scalability ,both wrt H/w as well as Software as a service.
	
Mobile:
	1.Physical device which enables you to telework.
	2.The computing power is restricted to the power of the physical device.
	3.The software has to be installed onto the local device for accessing.
	4.Mobile can be connected to cloud services as well.
